History

ALFULAIJ FAMILY

Al-Fulaij is one of the distinguished merchant families in the gulf region. The family was heavily involved in the trade between Kuwait and various countries in the Arabian peninsula, Asia and East Africa during the early 1900’s and had offices in several countries trading various commodities and precious metals. After the discovery of oil in Kuwait, The Late Mr. Yousef Al-Fulaij joining hands with other merchant families, worked towards the transformation of Kuwait to become the most developed country within the region during mid 1900’s and beyond. The Late Mr. Yousef Al-Fulaij was one of the founders and served as a board member of various private, governmental and non-profit organizations, such as National Bank of Kuwait, Kuwait Oil Tankers Company, Kuwait Airways, Kuwait National Petroleum Company, Kuwait Cinema Company, Kuwait Chamber of Commerce, Kuwait Red Crescent, Kuwait Municipality and many others.

The Founder

Since his graduation from Tulsa University in 1975, Mr. Khalid Al-Fulaij had a vision of establishing his wholly owned company, following the footsteps of his ancestors. However, prior to pursuing his dream, he decided to first join Kuwait Oil Company (KOC), where gained knowledge and experience working for one of the largest national oil companies in the world. Alongside his employment at KOC, Mr. Al-Fulaij was also entrusted with leading and sitting in the board of several government and semi- government entities operating in drilling, construction, maintenance and manufacturing. In 1992 Mr. Al-Fulaij became the Chairman and Managing Director of KOC until his retirement in 1998.

The Inception

From his office within the greater family’s company premise, Mr. Khalid Al-Fulaij started the journey. KYF was founded in 1999 as a general trading and contracting establishment. Immediately after its foundation, and due to Mr. Al-Fulaij’s reputation, numerous international companies showed interest to join hands with KYF to promote and develop their businesses in Kuwait.
